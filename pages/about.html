My own definition of myself: an overthinking heart.

Academic expectations set on me made me choose Engineering in Industrial Design as a university degree in 2010 but my gut was telling me to go for something more creative all along.
Throughout my degree though I took some classes in more artistic disciplines and decided to pursue a career in graphic design. I went ahead to work in several communication departments and advertising agencies and completed my education with a Master's Degree in Design and Art Direction at Elisava in Barcelona.
In 2020 I decided to go freelance so I could have more control over the projects and work philosophy of what I do and I haven't regretted it ever since.

MY APPROACH

Any project always starts with research and creating a strategy. This helps me find direction and give meaning to it. Then comes the creative stage and the time to go a bit crazy. Throughout this process I let myself explore but by having that solid strategic base I can always go back to it to ensure I'm on the right path.
